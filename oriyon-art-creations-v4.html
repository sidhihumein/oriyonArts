What Is a Live Counter?

A station where your guests become the creators

A live art counter is an interactive station set up at your event venue. Guests walk up, choose their design, create their own personalized item, and take it home as a keepsake. No waiting, no passive watching — pure participation.

Weddings

Tote bag printing, charm bars, live calligraphy — guests engage and leave with something beautiful.

Exhibitions

Live counters at exhibitions create brand recall and make visitors linger, engage, and remember your brand.

Corporate Events

The Founder

Anushka Sendre

Oriyon Art Creations was founded by Anushka Sendre on 9 July 2021 in Indore, MP.

What began as customized handmade art products, personalized gifting solutions, and creative artworks across 25+ art forms has evolved into a full creative experience brand — bringing interactive art moments into birthdays, weddings, corporate events, and community celebrations.

Anushka believes art becomes most meaningful when people can participate in it, personalize it, and carry it home as a memory. That belief shapes every experience Oriyon creates.
